About Me

Erika Agbabian MA, LMFT

I'm happy you're here!

  • I am a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist who is passionate about building supportive and authentic relationships with my clients. My specialties include anxiety, depression, relationship concerns, & trauma for clients of all ages and backgrounds. I earned my M.A. in Clinical Psychology from Pepperdine University and attended undergraduate studies at University of California Davis. I have a lot of experience working with teen and young adult populations and have specialized training in trauma-focused cognitive behavioral therapy (TF-CBT).

  • My style is warm, compassionate, & collaborative. I approach therapy from an authentic, person-centered lens and draw from a range of evidence-based treatments to tailor therapy to your unique identity, needs, and goals. My priority is to create a space where you feel heard, understood, and supported.

  • Emocean Counseling Services is based in California and services can only be provided to clients residing in California. All sessions are provided online via telehealth.

Specialties

The areas below represent my primary specialties; however, I work with clients experiencing a variety of concerns. Reach out to see if we'd be a good fit!

Anxiety Management

Learn practical tools to manage anxious thoughts and physical symptoms, challenge underlying core beliefs, and develop more adaptive patterns of thinking and behavior that supports greater stability and control.

Depression

Process heavy emotions and beliefs, reconnect with your sense of purpose and value, and gently shift patterns of thinking and behavior to cultivate renewed hope and vitality.

Relationship Issues

Strengthen communication, develop healthy boundaries, and explore attachment patterns to build meaningful, secure, and lasting connections.

Life Transitions

Navigate transitions with greater clarity and resilience by processing emotions that come with change, redefining identity and direction, and building adaptive coping strategies that support your next chapter.

Trauma

Start your journey towards healing at your own pace in a space where you are met with safety, compassion, and patience. Deepen understanding of how past experiences continue to impact the present, implement skills to reduce distressing symptoms, and reshape your story in ways that honor your resilience and restore a sense of empowerment.

Child and Teen Therapy

Provide your child or teen with additional support as they navigate family dynamics, peer challenges, identity development, and growing independence so they can thrive at home, succeed at school, and feel confident stepping into their future.

Explore Treatment Options

All services are provided online via telehealth to clients in California.

Individual Therapy

A safe space to reflect, process, and make sense of your experiences while fostering personal growth, resilience, and well-being.

Couples Therapy

A supportive space for couples to strengthen connection, improve communication, and navigate challenges together.

Child and Teen Therapy

A welcoming environment for children and teens to work through challenges and explore identity in a developmentally appropriate, fun, and engaging way.
