Frequently Asked Questions

Don't see the answer to your question? Reach out and I'd be happy to answer any questions!

  • How can I book a session?

    Schedule online or contact us by phone or email. Flexible times are available.

  • Are sessions online or in-person?

    At this time, sessions are only provided virtually via secure telehealth platform.

  • What forms of payment are accepted?

    I accept both insurance and private pay. Please see the home page for the list of insurance providers accepted.

  • Do you provide services to clients outside of California?

    As a clinician licensed in California, I can only provide therapy to clients who reside in California.

  • How do I know if this therapy is the right fit for me?

    Choosing a therapist can feel like a big decision. Book a consultation to discuss your goals, learn more about my therapeutic style, and explore whether my approach feels like a good match for you.
